KCPR Contacts

Our executive staff has changed, the following staff is active through June of 2010.

It is imperative that your correspondence reaches the appropriate party. KCPR has around 20 executive board members, each with their own particular job functions and responsibilities. Please browse the list to find exactly whom you need to contact at KCPR. If you cannot find your proper contact in this list, check each of the executives' responsibilities. We cannot stress this enough, some emails are going unread because they are still going to the wrong addresses.

I would like to have an album played on KCPR - Music Directors
I would like to run a Public Service Announcement on KCPR - Traffic or Production
I would like to organize a contest or giveaway on KCPR - Promotions Director
I am interested in becoming a volunteer DJ at KCPR - Air Staff Instructor
I would like to perform live or have an interview on KCPR - Program Director
I need an accounting representative - Business Director
I have a comment to make on the quality of programming on KCPR - Program Dir.
I would like to rent KCPR's sound system or mobile DJ services - Mobile Director
I have a question about the equipment at KCPR - Engineer
I have a question about this website or the live stream - Internet Director
