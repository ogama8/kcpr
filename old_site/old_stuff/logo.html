KCPR Logo Contest Guidelines

Guidelines:
- Logo must clearly depict our call letters and call numbers: "KCPR 91.3fm" or "kcpr 91.3fm"
- In addition, it may also include "San Luis Obispo", "Cal Poly Radio", or "Cal Poly."
- The logo should be no more than two colors in addition to black and white.
- The logo must not have gradations or shading.
- The logo must be original and not infringe upon any other established logos or designs.
- Please submit color print out of your design and completed entry form to the Logo Contest box at KCPR, which is located in the Graphic Arts Building at Cal Poly on the second floor, room 201. You may also include your design on floppy disk in PC format, but are not required to do so.
- Please make sure that your entry package is no larger than 11 inches x 14 inches.
- All entries become property of KCPR upon submission.
