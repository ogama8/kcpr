What is it: KCPR's pledge drive is our newly-revived spring quarter fundraiser.  As you know, KCPR is a non-profit community radio station run completely by student volunteer. We rely on the support of our listeners so that we may continue to broadcast our unique programs.

When is it: We will officially begin the pledge drive on Saturday, April 16th during Cal Poly's Open House.  The pledge drive will continue to run from 7:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. until Friday April, 22. Some specialty shows after 6:00 p.m. may also have pledge packages so be sure to also tune into your favorite evening programs.

How it works: You make a pledge, you get cool stuff.  It's that easy.  We have several pledge packages available for different pledge amounts from $20 and up. See below.

How to make a pledge: If you'd like to support KCPR, stop by our booth during open house or give us a call during pledge drive hours at  (805) 756-KCPR.  Stay tuned to 91.3 FM and KCPR.org for updates!
